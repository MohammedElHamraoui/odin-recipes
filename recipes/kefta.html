Moroccan Kefta

Description

Moroccan Kefta is a delicious and flavorful dish that features juicy grilled meatballs made with ground lamb or beef, seasoned with a blend of aromatic spices like cumin, coriander, and paprika. The meatballs are often served with a side of couscous, vegetables, or a tangy tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground lamb or beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • Olive oil, for grilling

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the ground meat, onion, parsley, cumin, coriander, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the egg and mix well until the ingredients are evenly combined.
  3. Shape the meat mixture into small, oval-shaped meatballs.
  4. Heat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat.
  5. Brush the meatballs with olive oil and grill for 4-5 minutes per side, or until cooked through.
  6. Serve the kefta hot with your favorite sides, such as couscous, vegetables, or a tomato sauce.
